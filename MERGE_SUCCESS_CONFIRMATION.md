### 1. Browser Cache 🔄
- **Most Common Issue**: Your browser was showing the old cached version
- **Solution**: Hard refresh your browser
  - Windows/Linux: `Ctrl + Shift + R` or `Ctrl + F5`
  - Mac: `Cmd + Shift + R`
  - Or try Incognito/Private mode

- GitHub Pages takes 1-2 minutes to rebuild after merge
- You might have checked too quickly

### 3. Wrong URL 🔗
